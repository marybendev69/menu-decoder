How It Works
From photo to shareable translated menu in seconds.
📷
Capture
Snap a photo of a menu, paste a URL, or upload an image. We handle paper menus, PDFs, and digital menu sites.
🌍
Translate
AI translates every dish with cultural context — not just words, but what to actually expect on the plate.
🐟🧀🌿
Decode
Emoji ingredient icons let you spot fish, meat, dairy, truffle, and vegetarian dishes at a glance.
💬
Share
Tap dishes to build your order, then send it via iMessage, email, or copy — perfect for the table.
